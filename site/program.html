The Program

The Sustainable Shift, week by week.

Below is the full 90-day arc: what we work on, how it stacks, and why the back half compounds harder than the front.

Weeks 1 to 4

Foundation

Assessment, anchors, baseline. The first month exists to install the system you'll lean on for the next 60 days.

W1

Onboarding + Movement Assessment

Video movement screen, body comp baseline, lifestyle audit. We identify the asymmetries and habit gaps before adding load.

W2

Foundation Lifts

Four-day full-body or upper/lower split. Sub-maximal loads, focus on bracing, breathing, bar path.

W3

Nutrition Framework

Protein floor, hydration target, plate template. No tracking yet, just architecture.

W4

Sleep + Recovery Protocol

Sleep hygiene checklist, HRV baseline, first deload. Get the engine right before you redline it.

Weeks 5 to 8

Build

Real load. Real progressions. The phase where transformations actually start to show.

W5

Progressive Overload Block

Linear progression on primary lifts. Undulating volume on accessories. RPE-capped, tracked weekly.

W6

Nutrition Periodization

Macros shift to match training phase. Calorie cycling around higher-volume days.

W7

Mid-Program 1:1 Review

Body comp + performance retest. Adjustments based on actuals, not assumptions.

W8

Sport / Aesthetic Focus

Accessory programming tuned to your goal. Power, hypertrophy, or capacity.

Weeks 9 to 12

Sustain

The exit ramp matters more than the workouts. You leave with the skill to run your own programming.

W9

Autoregulation

You start calling daily RPE targets and exercise selection. I review and refine.

W10

Travel + Event Protocols

The 20-minute hotel session, the wedding nutrition plan, the holiday block. Built before you need them.

W11

Maintenance Calorie Reset

Reverse out of the deficit. Calorie target you can hold for a year.

W12

Final Retest + Exit Plan

Body comp retake, performance retest, written exit plan with optional quarterly check-ins.

Sample Workout

What a session looks like.

This is a real Week 5 upper-body day. Times are conservative.

A
Primary, 12 min

Bench Press

  • 4 sets × 5 reps @ RPE 7-8
  • 3 min rest between sets
  • Tempo: 3-0-1-0
B
Accessory pair, 18 min

Row + Press Superset

  • B1: Chest-supported row, 3×10
  • B2: Half-kneeling DB press, 3×10
  • 90s rest between supersets
C
Finisher, 10 min

Arms + Carry

  • Curl + tricep pushdown circuit, 3 rounds
  • Farmer carry, 3 × 40 yards
  • Optional sled push, 5 × 20 yards
