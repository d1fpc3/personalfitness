The Shift

A 90-day system, not a 30-day stunt.

Three months because that's how long it actually takes for habits to compound and physiology to remodel. Each month builds on the last.

Methodology

Four pillars. No bro-science.

Every recommendation traces back to peer-reviewed research and 7+ years of clinical practice. If a TikTok trainer told you the opposite, that's why they're a TikTok trainer.

01

Progressive Overload

Linear and undulating periodization tuned to your recovery capacity. We track tonnage, RPE, and bar speed, not vibes.

02

Nutrition Periodization

Calories and macros shift with your training phase, not a fixed number. Real food. No supplement stack pitch.

03

Recovery and Sleep

HRV-guided deloads, sleep hygiene protocols, and stress regulation. The fastest gains come from what you do when you're not training.

04

Behavior Change

Clinical-grade habit design. Identity work, environment design, weekly accountability. The program ends. The habits don't.
