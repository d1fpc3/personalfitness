Why I started coaching outside the clinic.

The same gap kept showing up in my treatment room. Smart, driven people would rehab a knee, leave the clinic, and within a year be back with a different injury caused by the same root pattern. They didn't need more PT. They needed a coach who actually understood how their body would adapt over the next 12 months, not the next 12 minutes.

So I started building the program I wished I could hand them on discharge day. A 90-day system that treats the body like the long-running project it actually is. Sustainable means it survives a busy quarter, a vacation, and your 50s.

If you're already disciplined in the rest of your life and frustrated that your body isn't keeping up, we should talk.
